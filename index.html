HERE'S TIME LINE OF I.R SOEKARNO

  • 1901- Soekarno was borned

  • 1920 - Graduated from high school

  • 1926- Graduate from Institute Technology of Bandung

  • 1927- Founded the Indonesian National Party

  • 1929- Imprisoned by the Dutch in Sukamiskin prison.

  • 1993- moved out from Sukamiskin prison to bengkulu's prison

  • 1942- released from prison and went to Jakarta to build BPUPKI

  • 1945 - Soekarno gave the basic idea of the Indonesian state

  • 1945- Elected to be 1st President in Indonesia.

  • 1970- Died at the age of 67
